Set in Pokhara, 8 km from Fewa Lake, Pokhara Homes Hotel offers accommodation with a restaurant, free private parking, a bar and a shared lounge. Featuring a garden, the 3-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The accommodation provides a 24-hour front desk, room service and organising tours for guests. All guest rooms in the hotel are fitted with a flat-screen TV. Pokhara Homes Hotel offers a À la carte or Asian breakfast. The accommodation offers a terrace. World Peace Pagoda is 12 km from Pokhara Homes Hotel, while International Mountain Museum is 6 km from the property.
